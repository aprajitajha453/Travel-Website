DEGANVY, UK NATURE VIEW

Deganwy is a town, community and electoral ward in Conwy County Borough, Wales. It lies on the Creuddyn Peninsula, between the seaside resort of Llandudno and the walled town of Conwy. The town is situated on the estuary of the River Conwy, which flows into Conwy Bay, part of the Irish Sea.
